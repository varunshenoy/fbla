The largest professional interest club at Cupertino High School

Cupertino FBLA is the largest business student organization at Cupertino High School. We welcome students of all different backgrounds, interests, and skill levels to join our community and share in our passion for all aspects of business, including accounting, technology, and presentation. Our meetings take place on Thursdays at lunch.

Like our Facebook Page and if you are a member, join our Facebook Group!

Calendar

We attend the Bay Section Leadership Conference (BSLC), California State Leadership Conference, and National Leadership Conference (NLC) every year, as well as the Leadership Development Institute (LDI) and various tours of businesses, such as Google and Facebook, around the year. Moreover, we host study sessions to prepare members for upcoming competitive events. All of this information and more is showcased in our calendar.
